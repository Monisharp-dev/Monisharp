A Guide on How to Earn on Monisharp Platform

There are many ways to earn fast and easily on Monisharp. Don't let your money go to waste. Below is a breakdown of our top earning features:

Daily Check-In

This is the easiest way to start earning. We reward you with ₦10 each day you log in and claim your reward from the "Daily Reward" section on the sidebar.

Referral System

Our referral system is simple and tailored for students. To earn through referrals:

  • Copy your referral code and the app link from the “Refer” page.
  • Tell your friends about Monisharp and show them your withdrawal proof.
  • Ensure they register using your referral code and activate with just ₦200.
  • Once done, you’ll be rewarded instantly!

Task System

To earn through tasks:

  • Go to the “Task” page from the side menu.
  • Pick a task you like and follow the "How to Do" guide.
  • Submit your proof as required:
    • Text Proof – type your response as instructed.
    • Choose File – upload your screenshot or proof.

Tasks are approved within 24–48 hours. You'll receive payment if approved, or feedback if not.

Quiz System

Participating in quizzes is fun and rewarding. Here's how:

  • Click “Join QuizBrain Mini.”
  • Learn more from “What’s QuizBrain Mini” in the menu.
  • Pay ₦50 using task balance (faster) or direct transfer (may take time).
  • Answer simple questions — we reward both winners and non-winners!

Leaderboards are published every Saturday. All participants are credited directly to their bank accounts without charges.
