Why Plant a Seed?

"Plant a Seed" is a fun and rewarding earning system where users invest a little to gain more. It promotes consistency, hope, and smart risk-taking.

How it Works

  • Pay a seed amount (e.g., ₦100) to enter a seed round.
  • Each round takes exactly 6 participants.
  • 3 or 4 lucky participants receive bigger rewards!
  • Unlucky ones get a ₦10 consolation reward.
